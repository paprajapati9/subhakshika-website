Who Are We?


The NGO has been working for education,rehabilitation and

uplift of the underprivileged sections of the NGO by providing

opportunities for education ,by imparting employment

oriented vocational skills and distributing articles of basic

needs to the needy.






Our Origin


Shubhkakshika Educational Society was formed and

registered in June 1993 under Society Act of 1860 by

professors of Delhi university, school teachers,professionals

and others committed to social causes.





Vision and Mission


Upliftment and elevation of underprivileged and deprived

section of society

To contribute towards educated and empowered India

To rehabilitate the marginalised sections socially and economically

Love and care for every child
